Mouse commands

You can zoom and move the plotting area with the following mouse commands:

Mouse wheel:

You can zoom in by moving the mouse wheel towards you and zoom out by moving the mouse wheel away from you.

Dragging the mouse with the left mouse button pressed:

A drag-rectangle is displayed. When you release the mouse button, the plotting area is zoomed to the drag-rectangle, e.g. the contents of the drag-rectangle are shown.

Dragging the mouse with the right mouse button pressed:

A drag-rectangle is displayed. When you releas the mouse button, the current plotting area is zoomed out to fit into the drag-rectangle.

Dragging the mouse with the middle mouse button pressed:

The plotting area is moved with the mouse.
