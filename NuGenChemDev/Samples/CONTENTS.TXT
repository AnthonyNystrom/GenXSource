This directory (Samples\Organics) contains a collection of interesting
organic molecules.  There are:

-- some important biomolecules (alanine, chlorophyll, cholesterol,
   hemin, progesterone)

-- some sugars (glucose, sucrose) and flavours (menthol, isoamyl acetate)

-- some drugs, both helpful and harmful (ampicillin, aspirin,
   caffeine, ethanol, nicotine, Vitamin C)

-- three vitamins (niacin, thiamine, riboflavin)

All the above were made with the HyperChem model builder.
